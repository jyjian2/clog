About

Jia Yi Jian's Recipe Information System, is a full-featured website for the recipe information sharing. This information system is designed for students in University of Illinois at Urbana Champaign. Most students don't have a car so they went to super market once a week and cook at home by themselves. Students can find a recipe according to the ingredients they have in their refrigerator. This website is implemented using Django.
